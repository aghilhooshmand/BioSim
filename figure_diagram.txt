Data Flow:
Input: CLuB (400) + cBioPortal (4000) = 1,600,000 potential pairs
↓
BioBERT Semantic Processing
↓
Output: Filtered, ranked semantic matches

Key Innovation:
Traditional: O(n²) brute-force comparisons
BioSim: O(n) semantic similarity filtering
Result: 99.4% reduction in computational complexity
